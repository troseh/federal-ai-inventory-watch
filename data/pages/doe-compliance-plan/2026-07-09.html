Department of Energy (DOE) Artificial Intelligence (AI) Compliance Plan

Office of the Chief Information Officer

October 1, 2025
Estimated Read Time   min

The Department of Energy (DOE) Artificial Intelligence (AI) Compliance Plan  outlines the Department’s current and planned efforts to achieve consistency with all federal requirements in Office of Management and Budget Memorandum M-25-21. The plan details DOE’s strategies for strengthening AI governance, advancing responsible AI innovation, and managing risks from the use of AI through the Department’s AI Hub.
